The Best Movies According to Lubna

My top 3 Movies of all-time

Interstellar

This movie is a science fiction masterpiece. Apart from the mysteries of the universe, it protrays so many things such as human emotions, love, sacrifice and betrayal.

Grave of the Fireflies

The most heart wrenching anime movie I have ever watched. It shows us the darkest sides of the atrocities of war. Although this movie is quite old but it is timeless and a masterpiece anime movie.

Five Feer Apart

Sometime circumstances force a person to sacrifice their love for the sake of their loved one. Five Feet Apart teaches us that if we truly love someone we have to let them go. I think it is quite underrated but it is based on real incidents that is what makes it more heart-breaking.
